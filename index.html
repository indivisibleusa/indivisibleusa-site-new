Our Purpose

One Nation,
Indivisible.

We believe America is worth showing up for. In person. With people who haven't quit.

Faith

This country was built on something bigger than politics. We don't pretend otherwise.

Freedom

The right to live how you want, say what you mean, and build without asking permission.

Community

We show up in person. At events, in cities, with people who mean it.

Identity

Patriotism fell out of style. We think that's worth fixing. The Modern Patriot is young, shows up, and doesn't apologize for loving this country.

"Every morning, thousands of us show up at @pledgeofallegiancedaily. To honor this country. To remind anyone watching that we still stand for it."

Indivisible USA is a daily practice. Show up every morning. Keep the thread alive.
