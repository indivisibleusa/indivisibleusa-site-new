Indivisible USA

Flag Run Recap

Miami Beach on a Saturday morning. What a beautiful sight to see. 175 patriots on the sand with flags.

The Night Before

The night before, Teag Pure Joy McCoy drove in with his team. We broke bread together and prayed. We cohosted this event with them.

Building the Flags

In the days leading up to it, we built flags by hand. Half ours, half built by Teag and his team. Every one of them made carefully, thoughtfully, with love.

The Cold Plunge

We even customized our own cold plunge that read: "In God We Trust." What a beautiful sight.

The Morning Of

Before we stepped onto the sand, we gathered, we chatted, we smiled, we prayed together. We said the Pledge of Allegiance and we sang the national anthem.

On the Beach

Then we took it to the beach. A 5K running portion and a ruck and walk portion led by Miami Ruck Club. People from all walks of life turned and looked at the sea of red, white, and blue. Truly an outstanding sight to be seen. Smiles were given. Fist bumps were given. God bless you's were said.

This is what Indivisible USA is. Something real, with people who cherish this country. People who believe this country is destined for righteousness.

We are so excited to continue building Indivisible USA and to stand for unity and the healing of America. God is our center, and we can't wait to see where He leads us.

Miami Beach is our home base. The next one is coming back here. Reply and tell us about your experience or share your city — we're building toward events nationwide.
